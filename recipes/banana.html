Banana Bread

    Ingredients

  • 125 g butter, softened
  • 220 g brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 400 g mashed bananas
  • 250 g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • chopped nuts or dried fruit (optional and quantity to suit)

    Steps:

  1. Combine the butter, sugar, vanilla extract with an electric mixer until slightly paler.
  2. Beat in the eggs, then the mashed banana.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the remainder of the ingredients, then add to mixture.
  4. Spoon mixture into 12 cm x 25 cm loaf pan and bake in preheated oven at 160 degrees C for 1 hour.
