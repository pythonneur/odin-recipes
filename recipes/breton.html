Breton Cake

    Ingredients

  • 150 g salted butter, softened
  • 150 g sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 250 g flour

    Steps:

  1. Start by putting the butter in a bowl. Add the sugar and mix thoroughly.
  2. Then add the egg yolks, one at a time, mixing thoroughly for each. Save some of it to use as an egg wash.
  3. Similarly, add the flower a bit at a time and incorporate completely.
  4. Transfer the dough to a greased/buttered cake mold, about 20 cm in diameter.
  5. Pack the dough down to get a relatively flat and smooth surface. Use a fork to create lines.
  6. Dilute the leftover egg yolk with a bit of water and apply as egg wash.
  7. Preheat the over to 180 degrees C and bake for 40 minutes
