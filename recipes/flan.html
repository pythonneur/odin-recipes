Flan

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 ounces)
  • 1 can evaporated milk (12 ounces)
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

    Steps:

  1. In a medium size pot, melt the sugar until golden. Pour the syrup into a 9 inch diameter glass dish. Swirl the dish to coat the bottom and sides, then set aside.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs. Beat in the condensed milk, evaporated milk and vanilla extract. Pour into the glass dish and cover with foil.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and bake for 60 minutes. Allow to cool completely.
  4. Invert onto serving plate once completely cool.
